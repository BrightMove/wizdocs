Confidential Scoring of Fit for Purpose

25/100
Overall Fit Score for HRIS Requirements

Honest Assessment: BrightMove is a poor fit for a comprehensive HRIS RFP. While we excel in our core competency of applicant tracking and recruiting, we lack the fundamental HRIS capabilities that most organizations require.

Recommendation: We should decline this RFP or position ourselves as a specialized ATS component within a broader HRIS ecosystem, not as a primary HRIS solution.

Specific Strengths vs. HRIS Requirements

✓ Areas Where BrightMove Excels

  • Applicant Tracking: Industry-leading ATS with 20+ years of experience
  • Recruiting Workflows: Highly configurable hiring processes and approval chains
  • Job Distribution: Comprehensive job posting to 50+ job boards
  • Candidate Experience: Modern, responsive candidate portal and application process
  • Recruiting Analytics: Deep insights into hiring metrics and performance
  • EEO Compliance: Built-in equal opportunity reporting and compliance tracking
  • Integration APIs: Robust API framework for connecting with other HR systems
  • Customization: Highly configurable to match unique recruiting processes

Critical Weaknesses for HRIS

⚠ Major Gaps in HRIS Functionality

  • No Payroll Processing: Cannot handle salary calculations, tax withholdings, or payroll distribution
  • No Benefits Administration: No enrollment, management, or tracking of employee benefits
  • No Performance Management: No performance reviews, goal setting, or employee development tracking
  • Limited Employee Records: Only maintains candidate data through hiring, not comprehensive employee records
  • No Employee Self-Service: No portal for employees to access HR information or submit requests
  • No Time & Attendance: Basic time tracking only for staffing agencies, not comprehensive T&A
  • No Learning Management: No training, certification, or professional development capabilities
  • No Succession Planning: No career pathing or succession planning tools
  • No Organizational Management: No org charts, position management, or reporting structures
  • No Employee Lifecycle: Limited to hiring process, no onboarding, transfers, or termination workflows

Areas Requiring Clarification

Critical Questions for Customer

  1. Primary Need: Is the main requirement recruiting/hiring or comprehensive HR management?
  2. Current Systems: What HR systems are currently in place for payroll, benefits, performance?
  3. Integration Approach: Open to best-of-breed solutions or require single-system approach?
  4. Organizational Size: Number of employees and expected system users?
  5. Implementation Timeline: Urgency and phased vs. comprehensive deployment preferences?
  6. Budget Considerations: Budget allocated for HRIS vs. specialized ATS solution?

Strategic Recommendations

Option 1: Decline RFP (Recommended)

Rationale: BrightMove is fundamentally not an HRIS solution. Responding to this RFP could:

  • Waste customer's time with an inappropriate solution
  • Damage BrightMove's reputation by appearing to misunderstand our core competency
  • Create false expectations about our capabilities
  • Lead to implementation failure and customer dissatisfaction

Option 2: Reposition as ATS Component

Alternative Approach: If customer engagement is desired:

  • Clearly position BrightMove as the recruiting/ATS component of their HRIS strategy
  • Recommend partner HRIS solutions for core HR functions
  • Focus on integration capabilities and best-of-breed approach
  • Emphasize our ATS expertise while being transparent about limitations

Competitive Analysis

True HRIS Competitors

Organizations likely better suited for this RFP include:

  • Workday: Comprehensive HRIS with all core HR functions
  • BambooHR: Mid-market HRIS with recruiting, payroll, and performance management
  • ADP Workforce Now: Full-service HRIS with payroll and benefits
  • SAP SuccessFactors: Enterprise HRIS with complete HR lifecycle
  • Paycom: Single-database HRIS with payroll and HR management

BrightMove's Competitive Position

BrightMove would be competitive if this were an ATS-only RFP, but for comprehensive HRIS, we are significantly outmatched by true HRIS providers.
