RFI Doc7031886 - Applicant Tracking System (ATS)

Executive Summary

Inovium and BrightMove are pleased to present our joint venture proposal for the District of Columbia's Applicant Tracking System requirements. Our partnership combines Inovium's proven government implementation expertise with BrightMove's industry-leading ATS platform to deliver a comprehensive solution specifically tailored to DC government's unique hiring needs.

Understanding DC's Scale & Complexity:

  • Total DC Government: ~39,000 employees across all agencies
  • System Scope: ~20,000 employees (excluding DCPS, MPD, OCFO, UDC separate systems)
  • Pilot Implementation: OCTO, OCP, and DPR agencies (~600 employees, 87 HR staff)
  • Current System: PeopleSoft Federal v9.2 with eRecruit module (397 current users)

Our Joint Value Proposition:

  • Government Sector Expertise: Inovium's deep understanding of federal and municipal hiring processes
  • Proven ATS Technology: BrightMove's 20+ years of experience with complex government implementations
  • Phased Implementation: Pilot-first approach with OCTO, OCP, and DPR for validation before district-wide rollout
  • Mandatory Integration: Seamless careers.dc.gov API integration and PeopleSoft data exchange

Key Benefits for DC Government: Modernized hiring processes, improved candidate experience, enhanced compliance reporting, and scalable solution that grows from pilot (600 employees) to full deployment (20,000 employees).

Pilot Implementation Strategy

Phase 1 - Pilot Deployment (OCTO, OCP, DPR):

  • Scope: ~600 employees across 3 agencies
  • Users: 87 HR staff currently using eRecruit
  • Critical Success Criteria:
    • Seamless careers.dc.gov API integration
    • Successful import of existing HR staff accounts
    • Reliable export of hired applicant data to PeopleSoft
    • User satisfaction score >4.0/5.0
    • Time-to-fill reduction >20%

Phase 2 - District-Wide Rollout (upon pilot success):

  • Scope: ~20,000 employees district-wide
  • Scalability: Platform designed to handle 100,000+ employees
  • Proven Success: Pilot validation ensures smooth full deployment
