Additional Modules: - BrightForms: $99/month for unlimited forms - Wisdom Data Platform: $499/month (one-time setup: $499) - Back Office Module: $499/month (one-time setup: $499) - Engage Module (SMS): $999/month (one-time setup: $99)

  • System Setup: $499 (Wisdom setup) + $499 (Back Office setup) + $99 (Engage setup) = $1,097
