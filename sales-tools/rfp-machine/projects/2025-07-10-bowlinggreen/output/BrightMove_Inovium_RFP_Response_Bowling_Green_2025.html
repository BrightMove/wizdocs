Executive Summary

Inovium and BrightMove are pleased to present our joint venture proposal for the City of Bowling Green's Applicant Tracking System requirements. Our partnership combines Inovium's proven implementation expertise with BrightMove's industry-leading ATS platform to deliver a comprehensive solution tailored to your municipal hiring needs.

Understanding Bowling Green's Requirements:

  • Organization Size: 650 employees across city departments
  • User Requirements: 75 user accounts for departmental hiring decisions
  • Job Volume: 100 job postings annually
  • Hiring Process: Comprehensive municipal hiring with compliance requirements

Our Joint Value Proposition:

  • Specialized Municipal Experience: Inovium's deep understanding of government hiring processes and compliance requirements
  • Proven ATS Technology: BrightMove's 20+ years of experience with thousands of successful implementations
  • Comprehensive Service Delivery: End-to-end implementation, training, and ongoing support
  • Cost-Effective Solution: Competitive pricing with transparent, predictable costs

Key Benefits for Bowling Green: Streamlined hiring processes, improved candidate experience, enhanced compliance reporting, and significant time savings for your HR team managing 100+ annual job postings across 75 departments.

System Capabilities

Core ATS Functionality

Question Yes/No Response
Job posting to multiple job boards Yes Integrated posting to 50+ job boards including Indeed, LinkedIn, ZipRecruiter, and government job sites. One-click posting with brand customization.
Resume parsing and candidate profiles Yes Advanced AI-powered resume parsing that extracts and structures candidate information into searchable profiles with 95%+ accuracy.
Candidate screening and ranking Yes Automated candidate scoring based on job requirements, knockout questions, and weighted criteria. Customizable screening workflows.
Interview scheduling automation Yes Automated interview scheduling with calendar integration. Note: Scheduling is managed by recruiters, not candidate self-service.
Workflow/process steps per job posting Yes Fully customizable workflows with unlimited steps, approval processes, and automated status updates. Configure unique processes for different job types.
Job templates with pre-population Yes Comprehensive job templates with custom fields, requirements, and screening questions to reduce data entry and ensure consistency.
Free-form notes on applicants Yes Unlimited notes with date/time stamps and user attribution. Individual and bulk note capabilities with full audit trail.
Internal secured notes Yes Role-based note visibility with secure internal notes that can be restricted to specific users or groups.
Talent pool management Yes Comprehensive talent pool functionality allowing candidates to submit profiles without applying to specific jobs. Searchable talent database.
Candidate tags/keywords Yes Flexible tagging system with individual and bulk tag application. Tags can be used for talent pool segmentation and advanced searching.
Mobile app for users No No standalone mobile app. The platform is fully responsive and mobile-optimized for web browser access on all devices.
Internal vs external job posting Yes Full control over job visibility with internal-only postings, external-only postings, or both. Separate screening questions for internal candidates.
Automatic posting/unposting scheduling Yes Automated job posting and removal based on specified dates and times. Prevents expired postings and ensures timely job visibility.
Post-offer activity tracking Yes Comprehensive post-offer workflow tracking including background checks, drug testing, reference checks, and onboarding documentation.
Candidate self-scheduling No No candidate-facing scheduling tools available. Interview scheduling managed by HR staff through calendar integration.
Candidates apply without account creation No Candidates must create an account to apply for jobs. This ensures better candidate tracking and communication capabilities.
