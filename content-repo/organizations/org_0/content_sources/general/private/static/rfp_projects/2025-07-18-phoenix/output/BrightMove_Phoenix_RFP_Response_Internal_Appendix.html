Confidential Scoring of Fit for Purpose

25/100
Overall Fit Score for Phoenix Time and Labor Management System

Honest Assessment: BrightMove is a poor fit for the Phoenix Time and Labor Management System requirements. Our platform is designed specifically for applicant tracking and recruiting, not workforce time and labor management.

Core Issue: This RFP is seeking a comprehensive time and labor management system (time tracking, scheduling, payroll integration, labor compliance) while BrightMove is an Applicant Tracking System (ATS) focused on recruiting and hiring processes.

Key Strengths: Excellent recruiting capabilities, strong API integration, solid security posture, and proven track record in talent acquisition.

Major Concerns: Fundamental mismatch between RFP requirements and BrightMove's core functionality. We cannot deliver the time tracking, scheduling, and labor management capabilities requested.

Recommendation: Do not pursue this opportunity. The requirements are outside our core competency. Recommend focusing on ATS/recruiting opportunities instead.
